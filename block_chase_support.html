Thank you for downloading Block Chase! We're here to help you have the best experience possible. Below, you'll find resources and information to assist you.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • 1. How do I play the game?

    Use the swipe gestures to move your hero block across the grid and catch the blinking block.

  • 2. Do I need an internet connection to play?

    No, Block Chase is designed for offline play. You can enjoy the game anytime, anywhere.

  • 3. How do I update the game?

    Updates are provided through the App Store. Ensure your Apple Watch is connected to your iPhone and check for updates regularly.

  • 4. The game is not responding. What should I do?

    Try restarting your Apple Watch. If the problem persists, uninstall and reinstall the game.

  • 5. How do I contact support?

    If you need further assistance, please contact us at rajivsingh.github@gmail.com.

Troubleshooting Steps

  • Restart your Apple Watch:

    Press and hold the side button until you see the Power Off slider, then drag the slider. To turn it back on, press and hold the side button again.

  • Reinstall the Game:

    On your Apple Watch, press and hold the app icon until it jiggles, then tap the 'x' to delete. Reinstall it from the App Store.
