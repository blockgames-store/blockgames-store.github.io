TIC TAC POOF

About

Tic Tac Poof is a minimalist twist on the classic game of Tic Tac Toe. With a unique three-mark system and time-based challenges, every match becomes a strategic battle against time and position.

How to Play

• You can only have three marks on the board at once

• When you place a fourth mark, your oldest mark disappears

• Win by getting three of your marks in a line

• Each game has a three-minute time limit

Privacy

Tic Tac Poof is completely offline and does not collect or store any personal data. The game operates entirely on your device without requiring internet connectivity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does the game require internet connection?
No, Tic Tac Poof works completely offline.

Q: Is my data being collected?
No, we don't collect any personal data. The game runs locally on your device.

Q: How do I reset the game?
Tap the reset button during a game or start a new game from the menu.

Q: Is there a time limit?
Yes, each game has a three-minute time limit to keep matches quick and exciting.
